Frequently asked

  • How do the AI scans work? Tap the green AI button, choose or take a photo of a problem area, and you'll get a likely identification plus an organic and a conventional treatment plan. Your first 3 scans are free.
  • What is Big Grass Guy Pro? A monthly subscription that unlocks 25 AI diagnoses per month. You can subscribe or cancel anytime.
  • How do I manage or cancel my subscription? Open the Settings app on your iPhone, tap your name → Subscriptions, choose Big Grass Guy, and manage it there. Cancelling keeps Pro active until the end of the current billing period.
  • Why does the app ask for my location? Only to fetch your local weather forecast and give region‑aware advice. Your lawn journal, soil tests, and logs stay on your device.
